About Us

❝ Small Enough to Care – Big Enough to Cope ❞

Metro Bolts is a privately owned Australian company that has been successfully supplying fasteners for almost sixty years.

Originally established just after the Second World War as Motor Bolts Pty Ltd in Hawthorn, the business became more successful, requiring larger premises. The shift was made to 323, and then to 327-329 Swan Street.
